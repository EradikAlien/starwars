Beard One

A BeCode Story

Year 2022. It was a time of conflict, of uncertainty, a time of choice. Laurent discovered BeCode thanks to his girlfriend. He decided the time has come to change his career and to follow his father's steps in web development.


The first challenge was to get into the selection process by completing a few classes online, with a short deadline. The second challenge was the dreaded selection process. Facing juries and working side by side with a team to create a fake start-up. After successfully entering, the last challenge started...


One month to prove he was worthy of the training. It started with ice-breaking exercises to meet his new colleagues. Followed by familiarization with the terminal, git, Markdown and Github. To finally start working with html and css languages.
Now the future is ahead and everything depends only on him to prove himself...
